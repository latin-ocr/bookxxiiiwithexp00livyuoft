CHAPTER XLVI.

Romans victorious. Inflict great Loss on the Enemy, suffer but little
themselves. Marcellus burns the Spoils in Honour of Vulcan. Two
hundred and severity Horsemen desert from Hannibal. These sre-
quently render great and faithful Assistance to the Romans during
the War, at the Close of which they are rewarded. Hanno sent back
to the Bruttii. Hannibal goes into winter-quartem in Apulia. Q.
Fabius victuals and strengthens his Camp above Suessula. Ravages
the Campanian Territory with Fire and Sword. The Campaniam,
powerful in Cavalry, come outside Capua, and har-ass the Romans.
